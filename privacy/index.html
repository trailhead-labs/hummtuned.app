Plain and up front

Privacy.

Humm is built so there is almost nothing to write here. The app collects no personal data, needs no account, and asks for no special permissions. This website counts visits and nothing more. Here is the longer version, in plain words.

The app collects no personal data and runs entirely on your device.
No account, ever. Nothing to sign up for.
No tracking, in the app or across the web.
No dubious permissions: no location, microphone, or contacts.
This website uses cookieless analytics that counts aggregate visits only.
We never sell, rent, or share your data. There is nothing to sell.

On your phone: the app

Everything Humm does happens on your device. The programs you pick, the length you set, how you pace a session, and when you use it: all of it stays on the phone. None of it is sent to us or to anyone else. Humm works with no connection at all, because there is nothing it needs to phone home for.

No intrusive permissions. Humm shows no location prompt, no microphone request, and no access to your contacts or photos. What it uses is ordinary playback: background audio so a session keeps going with the screen off, Bluetooth headset controls, and the lock screen and Control Center or notification controls on iOS and Android. None of those move your data anywhere; they just let you play, pause, and listen. That is the full extent of what Humm touches, and we do not foresee it ever needing more.

No account. You never hand us an email, a name, or a password, because there is nothing to hand over and nothing for us to store.

Payment stays with the stores. Your free week and your purchase are handled by the App Store or Google Play under their own terms, not by us. We receive only the limited, aggregated sales reports the stores provide. We never see your card details.

On this website

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-respecting, cookieless tool, so we can see roughly how many people visit and which pages they read. It records aggregate figures like page views and referring sites. It sets no cookies, builds no profile of you, and cannot follow you to other websites. We use it to know whether the writing lands, and for nothing else.

There is no advertising here, no ad networks, and no social tracking pixels. We do not sell, rent, or share any of this with anyone.

What we never do

Sell, rent, or trade your data
Share it with advertisers or data brokers
Build a profile of you or your habits
Track you across other apps or sites
Email you unless you wrote to us first

Questions, or a request

Because we keep no account and almost nothing tied to you, there is little to request, correct, or delete. If you have a question about any of this, or a request about the aggregate website data, write to us at willem@leuver.ink and a person will answer.

Humm is not directed at children under 13, and we knowingly collect no data from anyone, at any age.

If this policy changes, we will update the date here and, for anything material, say so plainly. Humm is made by Trailhead Labs.
